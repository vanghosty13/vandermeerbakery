October 30, 1934 - 1997
Welcome to the VanderBlog at VanderMeerBakery.com
Pete Vander Meer Sr. opened the Vander Meer Bakery in LeMars Iowa on October 30, 1934. His son, Pete Vander Meer Jr. became a partner in the early 1960s and eventually bought the bakery from his father in the early 1970s. He ran the business until he retired in 1997, at which time he sold the bakery.
This site presents bakery memorabilia collected by Pete Jr. over the years. We hope you enjoy it.
